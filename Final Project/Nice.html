Nice

Nice is located on the south east coast of France right by the Mediterranean Sea. It is also one of the most popular places to visit in France

Some places that you can visit while being in Nice are:
  • Vieux-Nice
  • Castle Hill
  • Arena Mexico
  • Promenade des Anglais
  • Place Masséna


Safety tips and precautions while being in Nice

Every place that you go visit has it's pros and cons. One of the great things about Nice is you get to explore the cities and the towns around. One of the things that many tourist dislike about Nice is the beach. According to the tourist the beach is sloppy and rocky. Like any other trip that you make, you should where you are going to be staying at so you won't be finding a place. You should also have your transportation planned out.

Nice is a pretty safe place to go visit, but you should take some precautions. Some things that you should keep in mind is to not be walking around the city at night in the dark. Since you probably don't know around very much, you do not want to risk anything. This includes being at the beach at the night time. You should probably also not go to towns or places that do not look welcoming. Stick to going to places where they are several peope wandering around.
