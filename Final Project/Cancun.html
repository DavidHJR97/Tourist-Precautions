Cancun

Cancun is located in the state of Quintana Roo which is on the Yucatan Peninsula. Cancun is surrounded by many mayan sites. There are several things that you can do while being in Cancun.

Several places to go while being in Cancun are:
  • Coba
  • Tulum
  • Playa del Carmen
  • Isla Mujeres
  • Chichen Itza
In these places you can go snorkeling, go on a boat ride, climb a ruin, and many other things.

Safety tips while being in Cancun

Over the past couple of years, the violence is Cancun has gotten worse due to the drug cartels and the drug war.

While being in Cancun it's best to be in groups. Whether you are in a club or on a excursions, do not seperate from the people you are with. Always carry dollars and pesos. People are going to ask for a tip whether its your driver, server, bartender, or the crew that was on the boat.

Traveling Tip & Precautions

Make sure to plan ahead and book everything on time. Especially when you are at the Cancun airport, you want to have your taxi or who ever is going to take you to your hotel or airbnb there ready for you. There are many companies that will offer you take to you, but its best to have someone already there. You also don't want to be in line waiting to rent a car. Any paper work that they give you, make sure to fill it out completely and dont' lose anything that they give you at the airport.
