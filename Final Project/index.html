Traveling

Each year there are millions of people that travel across the world to several different locations. These locations include Europe and Central America. To be specific, many people go to Mexico and France.

There are many things to do in these countries but there are also things that you can not do. Some things to consider when visiting other countries is to be aware of your sourroundings and don't do anything that is disrecpectful to the cultures and the people.

Mexico

Mexico is a very popular place for tourist to go visit. One of the great things about Mexico is that there are several cities that you can go visit. Two of the great cities that are in Mexico are Cancun and Mexico City.

Cancun is a place that will make you not want to leave because of all the things that you can do. Mexico City is a place full of historic sites that you do not want to miss out. Will these great cities there are also things that you should be aware of when visiting.

France

France is one of the most popular countries to go visit in Europe. France also has plenty of cities for tourist to go visit. The most popular is the capital of France, which is Paris. Another city is France that tourist go is Nice.

There are many wonderful things to visit while being in Paris like the Ellief Tower and the Louvre museum. Nice has a beautiful sea view that you would not regret seeing. You should also be aware of the risk that comes when traveling to France.
